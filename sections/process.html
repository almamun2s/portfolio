Process

Find out how I work

First, I make a conversation with my clients. It is very important for me. Because, it is nedded to understand the project requirements of them. Sometimes, I make audio or video call to communicate with clients faster

Planning

Second, I make a better plan for coming project according to client's requirements. Every single details is listed in the plan. It helps me to compleate the work faster and to understand the clients about their project's progress.

Design

Design Quality is very important for every website. I value simple content structure, clean design patterns, and thoughtful interactions. I like to code from scratch, and enjoy bringing new and unique ideas to websites.

Coding

Websites are built by web language that's why we need to code for every website. It's very important to have hand, clean and developer friendly coding. I give guarantee in all of them.

Review

After designing and coding, I send for review to client. After client's checking, If have to change or revision, I say client to send all of them in a list and I am happy to do all changes every time

Ready

After reviewing everything, we are going to the next step, if everything has done, then I can publish it for everyone. In every project, i make sure to give full support to the clients.
